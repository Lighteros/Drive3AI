How It Works

Your Nodes Power the Drive3AI Network

Thousands of people around the world just like you contribute their spare computing power to store and deliver media to dApps running on the Drive3AI Network. In return, they are rewarded with Drive3AI tokens.

Smart Storage

Hard Drive

By contributing spare storage on your hard drive, you enable Drive3AI Network to store media and data in a fast, secure and decentralized manner.
Distributed Delivery

Network Card & Bandwidth

Your spare network bandwidth is utilized to send media and data, via your Drive3AI Node, to users and dApps on Drive3AI Network. This method ensures lightning fast delivery whilst retaining high data quality.
Transcoding & AI Computation

CPU & GPU

Your Node performs optimal formatting of media files, known as transcoding. Nodes also power AI computation within the W3AI ecosystem.
